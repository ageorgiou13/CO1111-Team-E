Treasure Hunt

Play


You can start playing after pressing Play button. When you press it you will be able to select the treasure hunt you want to play, after selecting you provide your username and you start

Scoring System


Each question gives points to the player. When the player answers wrong , loses 3 points.

Skip


Some questions can be skipped with the use of Skip button, but you lose 5 points.

Unfinished Treasure Hunt


If you left your treasure hunt unfinished, you can continue by simply pressing Play button and then Continue Session button

Leaderboard

On Leaderboard, you can check top 15 players
